About Paradise Event Arena

Our Story

Paradise Event Arena has been a cornerstone of Lagos event hosting since 2025. Founded with the vision of creating a premier venue that could accommodate events of all sizes, we have hosted thousands of memorable occasions from intimate gatherings to large-scale corporate events.

Our state-of-the-art facility boasts cutting-edge technology, versatile event spaces, and a dedicated team of professionals committed to making your event a resounding success.
