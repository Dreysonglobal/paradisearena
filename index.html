Welcome to Paradise Event Arena

Are you searching for the perfect venue to host your next event in Lagos? Look no further! Paradise Event Arena is strategically located at Alagomeji Bus stop, opposite OAK place, along Murtala Muhammed Way, Yaba, Lagos.

Our State-of-the-Art Facilities

Spacious Event Hall

A versatile event space that can accommodate:

  • 1,000 guests in banquet style. This space can be partitioned into 2 equal halves of 500 seater each.
  • 2,000 guests in theatre style can also be accomodated.

Immersive Visual Experience

Equipped with a large display screen for projections on both sides of the hall.

Lighting and Sound

Equipped with truss and fog machine for lighting props, stages on both sides, and top-notch sound systems.

Comfort and Convenience

Well-ventilated with strong cooling systems, very large and spacious rest rooms with latest facilities, and support staff to ensure a seamless experience.

Security

24/7 security with CCTV cameras and police presence, giving you peace of mind.

Additional Amenities

Changing rooms, kitchenette, and extremely large parking area for your guests' convenience.

Flexible Event Space

Our hall can be partitioned into two separate areas to accommodate multiple clients simultaneously.

Prime Location

The event centre is very accessible to both mainland and island clients with good navigational routes.

Whether you're planning a wedding, conference, concert, or any other event, our expert team is dedicated to ensuring your experience at Paradise Arena Event Centre is unforgettable.
